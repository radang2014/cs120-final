Discover the fun of exercise
Together.

What's the point?

Keeping the community healthy by encouraging exercise is our number one priority. We believe that when you find a form of exercise that gives you enjoyment and allows you to work out consistently, you can confidentally take control of your health, and become your best self!

How do we work?

Each user has the ability to create events for others in the area to see and join. So, this gives you the option to join someone else who is doing a similar work out to you, or to make your own if nothing catches your eye. Go to browse events to find what other people have created, or go to create new events to make your own!

Why with others?

Exercising is not the only way to strengthen a community, the bonds that people have with each other are just as important! While keeping yourself physically strong, meet others along the way that push you and keep you mentally strong as well. Plus, it's way more FUN.
